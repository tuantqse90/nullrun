Why move-to-earn keeps failing

Rewarding activity is easy. Rewarding real activity is the whole problem.

01

Move-to-earn collapsed under fraud. GPS spoofers, shake bots and emulator farms drained the reward pool — StepN-style economies paid the cheaters and died.

02

Loyalty rewards spend, not health. Points-for-purchase programs have no idea whether a member is actually active — so they can't reward the behavior brands and insurers really want.

03

Mobility apps are pure utility. Paying a toll or topping up VETC is a transaction with zero engagement loop — no reason to come back, no behavior to shape.

What Null Run is

A neutral engine that verifies real behavior, then lets partners reward it.

Activity is auto-captured on-device, distance is re-computed server-side (the client is never trusted), and only clean sessions mint soulbound points that convert to real rewards — starting with Guardian, Vietnam's ~3.5M-member health & beauty retailer.

Capture

Native, not a webview

Background GPS + Core Motion on iOS. Reliable sensor data is the product — it's what anti-cheat stands on.

Verify

Server-authoritative

Every fix is re-segmented and re-measured on the backend. Manually-added activity earns nothing. Genuine-only, by design.

Reward

Real money, not a token

Points are soulbound and non-transferable — no speculation, no farm-to-cash. They redeem for partner-funded rewards.

Tasco VETC Mobility · AI-Powered Gamification

The same engine, pointed at mobility.

Because verification is decoupled from the reward source, VETC becomes just another input. A source-agnostic adapter ingests mobility events and the gamification engine does the rest — no rebuild.

Adapter

Any event, one pipeline

Toll, top-up, fuel and parking events flow through a partner adapter into missions — idempotent per partner & external id, users matched by phone.

Aligned incentives

Rewards off-peak, never "more"

Mobility missions reward avoiding rush hours (6–9 / 16–19). We never pay drivers to drive more or faster — the incentive matches Tasco's congestion goals.

Safe AI

The model phrases, never invents

AI only rewrites real stats, picks from whitelisted mission templates, or writes copy over a deterministic churn score. The server clamps & persists every value.

Insight

Turns a member's real weekly numbers into a one-line, on-brand nudge — no hallucinated figures.

Personalize

Picks the next mission from a whitelist based on actual behavior; the server writes the override.

Win-back

Composes re-engagement copy on top of a churn score the backend computes deterministically.

Every AI endpoint falls back to templates with no API key set — the demo never depends on a live model.
What's hard to copy

The moat is trust, not features.

Anyone can hand out points. The defensible part is proving the activity was real, keeping the currencies from leaking into each other, and never touching the user's raw data.

Anti-cheat from day one

Genuine-only earning

7 weighted signals score every finish; speed, gap and accuracy outliers are dropped before distance is even computed. Only clean sessions mint. Rate caps + App Attest sit underneath.

Economy firewall

Currencies can't leak

Guild XP, caught pets, body-scan progress and mobility glory are cosmetic — they never touch personal points or partner rewards. It kills the farm → power → cash-out flywheel before it starts.

On-device only

Privacy is the product

The 3D body scan and street-pet photos are processed with on-device Vision and never leave the phone — excluded from backup, never uploaded. ZK-attested achievements are the next layer.

What's actually running

One Rust engine, a native app, our own maps — all deployed.

Backend
100% Rust monolith — Axum + sqlx over Postgres/TimescaleDB, Redis streams for GPS ingestion and leaderboards. Compile-checked SQL, no ORM.
iOS
Swift + SwiftUI, native — background GPS, on-device Vision for body scan & animal recognition, a real 3D hologram of your silhouette. No download models.
Maps
Self-hosted vector tiles — MapLibre over our own styles at maps.nullshift.sh. We render the run route; we don't rent it.
AI
OpenAI-compatible via reqwest — insight / personalize / win-back, each clamped by the server and template-safe when offline.
Web
Next.js partner & VETC console — reconciliation, mission tuning and AI copy review at run.nullshift.sh. Not the consumer app.
The model

Sell trusted engagement. Let partners fund the rewards.

Null Run is B2B2C: we provide the neutral activity + verification layer and first-party health signals; Guardian, VETC and — later — insurers and brands plug in and fund what their members earn. The engine is built once; every partner is a new input.
